What is Better Engagements?

Better Engagements is the UK and Ireland's transformation programme that will change the way we manage our client engagements – unlocking benefits for our people, our business and our clients. Find out how.

The Little Book of Better Engagements is a useful guide for those who want to know more about Mercury, our global, integrated business solution. It is designed to act as a reminder about Better Engagements, the Value Opportunities and the key steps to using Mercury.

What is Mercury?

EY’s global integrated business technology solution – Mercury – will be implemented on 14 October 2019 in the UK and Ireland, replacing a number of our current systems.

What is changing with Better Engagements and Mercury?

  • Opportunity and Engagement Lifecycle and new ways of working
  • Contact Management and Opportunity Creation
  • Pricing
  • Budgeting, Engagement Economics and Maintenance
  • Billing and Reporting
