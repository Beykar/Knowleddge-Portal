As we prepare for go-live, we all need to understand what this means for our ways-of-working. There will be a significant change to the way we think about and manage our client engagements. As a Client Server, you will be responsible for running and managing your opportunities and engagements in Mercury. Therefore, it is fundamental you complete your relevant learning to better understand how this impacts you.

Web Based Learning (WBL) modules are critical to your learning as these will explain the fundamentals of what’s changing with Mercury. In addition to WBLs, we are providing classroom-based learning for those of you who have been specifically nominated to participate by your Service Lines.

Below you will see an overview of the different components of the Better Engagements and Mercury learning programme. In addition to the information below, based upon your role, you may receive targeted e-mails about specific courses you are required to undertake. You should expect these e-mails from June 2019 onwards.
