Engagement Lifecycle: {{titleTitle}}

An Opportunity is defined by the widest contract agreed upon with a client or prospect. Wherever it is the client's expectation that EY behaves and communicates as 'One Team', regardless of SL/SSL and geography, we should place all the work under a single Opportunity.

An Opportunity is always required to start a risk assessment or to create an Engagement in Mercury.
